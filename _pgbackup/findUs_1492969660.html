RHEMA LIFE MINISTRY

At Rhema Life you will receive fresh vision, new revelations and spiritual gifts from God that will revolutionize your life. Come to your place of victory, a place where you will find peace for a new start in your Christian journey.

Rhema Life Ministry is a place where the power of God is released to meet your daily needs. Now located at 2700 West Oakland Park Blvd., Rhema Life is a ministry where the word of God comes alive in your Spirit, and a place where you will experience deliverance, healing, breakthrough and restoration.
