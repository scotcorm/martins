Our Ministry

Rhema life Ministry is a place where you will always witness the faithfulness of God. God has taken us on a wonderful journey. When we look back at the past few years, we could testify of stabilization and strategic advancement in the kingdom. Our ministry has grown in size and diversity, our leadership structure has changed, we have a much better established Sunday classic Ministration, Bible study, small group meetings etc that emphasis on care and spiritual growth, and we continue to see development in our other various ministries. Of course, the greatest thrill is watching God change lives. Some who are part of our church family today were not even followers of Jesus months back. Rhema life has been a good ground to walk in seeing souls won and lives changed to the Glory of God.

How God Works In Us

It is still God's policy to work through us and change our world and create in us a fulfilling future. This is one of life's greatest mysteries to us: that God would choose us to work out his change in this world through us. And it is this great zeal that God has called us to work in Rhema life Ministry. The early followers of Jesus believed this about themselves. In Acts 1:8 they were promised power when the Holy Spirit came upon them, and that they would be Jesus' witnesses in their city, region, and all around the world. They knew first-hand that the gospel, a relationship with Jesus, was the power of God to transform all who believe. That is why they were called "the ones who turned the world upside down" in Acts 17:6.

We at Rhema life have been given that same authority to transform lives in our region and the world in general. The degree to which we are changing our region and our world for God has indicated the degree of our effectiveness over the vision that is before us. We see lives changed, families changed, neighborhoods, workplaces, and communities impacted because of our presence.

What makes Rhema Life Unique?

Just as every person God creates is unique and has some very special qualities, so it is with churches as well....At Rhema Life, one of the things that make us unique is our commitment to three core goals.

When I became a pastor, I really didn't know what to do. However, in struggling to find out what I should really focus on, as a pastor, the Lord spoke to me to do these three things and build a church upon three simple goals. He said in essence, "if you want to build a great church that will always grow, then do these three things: Love people, unconditionally. Teach people, uncompromisingly. And Encourage people, unceasingly. It is upon these core foundations that we welcome you to the Rhema Life family! This means focusing on three different-yet-intertwined areas of our mission.

Our Relationship With You

We Love People Unconditionally.

Before Jesus ever did a miracle, preached a sermon, or died for our sins, God spoke to him and said, "This is my Son, whom I love. I am pleased with him!" (Matthew 3:17Isv)...” In a performance oriented world, it is refreshing to know there is still a place where love is unconditional and love never fails! No matter whom you are or what you've done, you are loved by us at Rhema life Ministry! We hope you experience that love with us.

We Teach People Uncompromisingly

As we teach the unchanging truths of God's Word, and you follow the principles and promises of scripture, you will receive the power to be changed forever! The Bible will mature you in your walk with God, equip you to fulfill God's purpose and empower you to experience victory in every area of your life, So that you will continue to receive breakthrough in all you endeavors.

We Encourage People Unceasingly.

We want you to know that God is not mad at you. He is not "mad about you"! He is on your side! No matter what you're going through, you are going to make it! Not a day goes by where this ministry does not pray for you. Not a day goes by where we are not available to encourage you, build you up and help you reach the "finish line" of God's perfect will over your life!

Our Story

Everything God does begins with a seed...and years ago, God planted in the heart of Rev Donbruce a vision for a church that would teach people the practical, life-changing principles of the Bible. Out of obedience to God and his passion to share God's unconditional love for people, Rev Donbruce moved to Singapore and began preaching the Word of God. What was first incorporated as a church in June of 2005, has now grown into today's Rhema Life Ministry --one of the fastest growing and diverse churches around the world, we have our Headquarter in Boca Raton, Florida, and our Asia Headquarter in Singapore. We have also started new satellite Ministries in Africa, China, Australia, Canada, and France

Over the past years Rhema Life has lived up to its name by seeing untold thousands of lives changed. Through thriving church programs, an expanding ministry, Community Life, outreaches and missions, the impact is far-reaching with no end in sight!

Our newly strategies ministry on campus will serve as the launching pad for us to birth satellite campuses around South Florida , and throughout the world, we have started a Rhema school of Ministry to train leaders, and also allowing us to continue changing lives and reaching nations with the Father's love, by teaching the gospel of Jesus Christ. Rhema life Ministry is reaching people and changing lives through the understanding of God's Word, building them into the likeness of Jesus Christ.

As Rhema Life Ministry joyfully looks forward to the next few years ahead, we would consider it an honor to have you be a part of what God is doing in Rhema life today. What makes us unique is the Vision, mission, that God gave us.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to bring people, breed them and build them to come together into their inheritance that God has predestined for them. We teach the word of God so that they will fulfill God’s dream in a united common purpose. We are led by an identified leader who is assisting us in our progress toward full devotion to Christ by intentionally providing an environment for connection, community and spiritual formation for the glory of God. We want you to experience the transforming power of Christ’s love. We want to prune you for the effectiveness of the gospel in building you according to your areas of calling with a view to raise church planters and church workers for world’s end ­time harvest. 2 Corinthians 5:17, John 3:16, Eph 4: 11-14

Our Vision

Our Vision is to make disciples from one generation to the next. To become a church where nobody stands alone. We want people to know their right standing in life and to become what God has predestined and planned for them. A place, where there is always a seat for everybody in respective of who they are, where they come from. We are the body of Christ, the family of God, a community of followers devoted to Christ and his cause, a glorious church, not having spot, or wrinkle, or any such thing; but that we should be holy and without blemish. A community of praise. We want to see the entire church comes together as one big family of God. Ephesians 5:27 Psalms 145:4, Matthew 28:19-20

Our Purpose

At Rhema life, our purpose is to prove to God our desire to achieve that goal. We must pursue it. Our purpose is that Christ may dwell in our hearts through faith that we being rooted and grounded in love, may have power to comprehend with all the saints what is the breath, length, height and depth and to know the love of Christ Church that surpasses all knowledge that we may be filled with all the fullness of God. That we may so therefore teach all nations, baptizing them in Name of the father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all things whatsoever God have comprehend us. Eph 3:17-19, Matthew 28 19-20.
