Welcome to Rhema Life Ministry

Thank you for visiting our website! We are excited that you took time to find out about Rhema Life Ministry. We like to say at Rhema Life, we are committed to serving God and meeting the needs of you and your family. We would also want you to know that RHEMA LIFE EMPOWERMENT OUTREACH CENTER is our empowerment center where you can find all the various programs we offer to the communities around us.

Using universal principles of spirituality, we offer practical tools for living a joyous, abundant and peaceful life. We believe that every person is valuable to God and, therefore, valuable to us. Our mission is to create environments for people to find their God-given purpose with acceptance, encouragement, and love.

Wherever you are on this spiritual path, you will find that supportive community with a wealth of opportunities for your personal growth at Rhema Life Ministry.

We aspire to strengthen each person's confidence in Jesus Christ at every point in their life and encourage spiritual growth through prayer. We do this by providing a multitude of opportunities for you to plug in, to serve and be served.

Whether your passion is in small groups meeting, Digging Deep into His Word , or making new friends; if you seek cutting-edge Student and Children's Ministry programs, dynamic music, community outreach, drama, or evangelism, there is a place for you to connect at Rhema life Ministry.

Kindly take your time to enjoy all our online materials, thanks again for stopping by our site. We invite you to return, because there are new updates here each week. If you have any question that isn't answered on our website, don't hesitate to contact the Church Office at any time (561) 451-4176. We can't wait to see you at one of our program. Come and visit for we care and you are going to love it when you visit.

What to Expect

When you Arrive

There are friendly greeter at the door who will be glad to assist you should you have any questions or special needs regarding seating or other general questions. Should any special needs arise during the service, feel free to go to any of our ushers to request assistance.

During Each Service

We welcome our first time guests. We won't put you on the spot or call you to the front, but we do ask that you fill out a visitor card and place it in the hands of our usher; we hope to call you in less than 72 hours of your visit. It also helps us keep record of your visit and connect you to our prayer network ministry worldwide.

Our Worship Service Goal

Is to inspire, encourage, stir and move you towards a potential Spiritual encounter with God. Through the power of the Holy Spirit, we hope to reach a sight and sound generation using music, teaching and media - you name it. We want to affect all your senses, reminding us all what a BIG God we serve!

After the Service

You would be invited to meet the visiting pastoral Staff who would introduce you to our various programs. We will greet you with goodies and also would like to treat you with some snacks in our foyer after the service, fellowship with you and get to know you the more, and look forward to answer any questions you may have. We endeavor to make you feel at home and make you stay at home. We would want to build and establish Gods victory around you, allowing God to enter into your life through our various programs and community outreach upon which if God is leading you to join our Rhema life family we would be glad to have you with us on this Spiritual Journey.

Our Prayer...

Our prayer is that this website will serve as a tool to share great news of the Gospel of Jesus Christ to you. Our mission is to impart this message by:

  • Exalting God.
  • Encouraging Christians toward spiritual maturity.
  • Equipping Christians for Ministry.
  • Evangelizing the World for Christ.

Our Family

It is upon these core foundations that we welcome you our Rhema Life Family! Join us today and you will experience a supportive community with a wealth of opportunities for your spiritual and personal growth. We look forward in meeting you!

Core Concepts

As you browse through our website, you will discover the three core foundations of which you are fully accepted to Rhema Life Ministry:

  • That we love people unconditionally.
  • That we teach people uncompromisingly.
  • And that we encourage people unceasingly.
  • Evangelizing the World for Christ.
